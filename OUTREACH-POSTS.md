## Tone Notes

- **Be a player first, founder second.** Every post should sound like it's from someone who plays Commander, not someone selling software.
- **Conviction is convincing.** Don't hedge with "maybe this could work." Say what you believe: "deck trading should be free and local."
- **Specific numbers > vague claims.** "12 cities, 87 decks" beats "growing fast."
- **Acknowledge the stage.** "We're early" is fine. Don't fake traction. Real people respect honesty.
- **The problem is the hook.** Lead with the buylist-retail spread pain. Every player has felt it.
- **Social proof compounds.** Each week, update the city count and deck count in new posts. Week 4 posts should reference the traction from Weeks 1-3.
